BARCELONA

FC Barcelona well known as "Barca" is a football team that was founded in (1899) based in Barcelona Spain.
The football club is well known for producing the best players in history such as Lionel Messi who is well known as
"THE GOAT" in this recent times and Xavi Hernandez who is the current manager. The team is also know for its 'tiki-taka'which is their form of playing involving short quick passes and movement of ball.
